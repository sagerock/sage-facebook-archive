This is Chris. I’m paying him $5/bag for cleaning up trash on the side of our hill. The most effective way to employ homeless people is through piece work. No set schedule. No required amount. Work when you want, how much you want. People that have lived on the street for years are some of the most free minded people you’ll ever meet. A traditional job is credibly challenging for many of the people in this community.
