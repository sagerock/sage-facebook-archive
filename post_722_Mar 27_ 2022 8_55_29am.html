I was standing in line at the donut shop. A church walked in to pick up their $450 order of 79 dozen donuts. The Church of the Nomadic Spirit will never buy $450 worth of donuts. I brought $10 worth of donuts and got another 3 dozen out of the dumpster. I'd get ALL our donuts from the dumpster but I want to thank this donut shop for allowing us to get their donuts out of the dumpster. It's a beautiful contribution to the community.
