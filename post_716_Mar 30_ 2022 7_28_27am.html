I LEARNED WHY AMHA VOUCHERS ARE EXPIRING IN AKRON OHIO

AMHA WOULD ONLY GIVE HER $800 FOR THE HOUSE.

I've been told houses have gotten condemned by allowing AMHA to come in.

YET AGAIN, the system is rotten and out of touch.
