I believe houseless people are on to something. There is a reason some people are on the street for years. There is a reason the movie Nomadland's van life was so compelling. There is a reason campgrounds are completely booked up a year in advance.

Houses cost a million dollars. Young people and old people can't afford "the American Dream."

There was a time. A very long time. When we were nomads. I think some of us are waking up to that memory.
