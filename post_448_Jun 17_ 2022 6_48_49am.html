I am EXTREMELY thankful we are talking about homelessness in Akron Ohio again.

I’ve said it before (a thousand times) and I’ll say it again: If you don’t like how I’m sheltering people then WHAT IS YOUR SOLUTION?

Because leaving them ignored in the woods is not a viable “solution.” It creates endless trash. Diseases are rampant becomes of no running water, toilets or trash service. And it’s all happening on land these peoples are illegally using.

You can hate homeless people and agree with me that is not the answer. But right now that IS the answer. And it’s wrong on every imaginable level.
