TRASH

The Beacon Journal article about how understaffed and under funded Keep Akron Beautiful is, has been weighing heavily on me.

I work more in trash removal than anything else I do in the homeless community.

I feel like trash is the physical manifestation of a mind that has been broken by a lifetime of trauma, sorrow, and hopelessness.

If we want to begin the healing of Akron we must deal with the trash.
