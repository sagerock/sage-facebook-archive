A homeless person is often the living embodiment of God.

Sad because of humanity's inability to conjure love and compassion and kindness when it is most important to do so. Hopeful because of humanity's ability to grow and learn, eventually. Angry because of humanity's cruelty and ignorance through it all. All while sharing every last item they have with anyone who needs it.
