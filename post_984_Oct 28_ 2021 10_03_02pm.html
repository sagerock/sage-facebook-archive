Isn't it "funny" how the idea of God, whose very definition is All of All, becomes a divider among humans? It's such a prevelant reality that it makes me think there must be something to it.

God connects and divides. Or maybe God connects and man divides. Maybe that's why we are here on Earth. To practice the truth of connection. Or maybe we are just a reflection of God. Both wonderful and terrible. Maybe we are just here to amuse God like fish in an aquarium.
