This is Tim. He has just gotten out of prison recently. I never ask why somebody was in prison. All I care about is who they are today. This guy is highly read and highly educated. I have a lot of interesting observations of this guy. And I really really like him. But the thing he was talking to me about today was how the pilgrims came here because they were tired of being oppressed for their beliefs. They came here to live their lives the way they wanted to live their lives. And ironically now we have people here at the Church of the Nomadic Spirit who are living their lives the way they want to live their lives. Harming absolutely no one. American citizens from generations of Americans. And the government is now telling them they can't live the way they want to live. We never seem to learn the lessons of history.
