A couple was brought to me by Ministry in Motion F.I.T-Friends in Tents
They had been evicted this morning with absolutely nowhere to go. They never had lived in a tent before. The woman, particularly, looked nervous. I've seen the look before. It's a look of like waking up to a nightmare you never imagined possible.

Meanwhile, the Akron budget meetings are going on now. They discussed evictions this week. The belief is that we are going to be back to pre-pandemic levels which amounts to 10 evictions every single day. That's over 3800 evictions a year.

Akron is number 1 in evictions in Ohio.
