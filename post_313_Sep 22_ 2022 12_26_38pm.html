I am happy to have the police gps monitor my travels, listen to my phone calls, go into any of my properties. Whatever. I am an open book.

He then asked me, "Why would they say that about you?"

I have been accused of every imaginable crime by homeless people. It usually happens because I make them mad for one reason or another. And then they just start saying stuff.

I AM NOT A CRIMINAL. I can't think one single thing I do that is illegal. My wife even gets annoyed that I follow the speed limit. "Arrive alive" is my motto.

I will keep you up to date on this issue.
