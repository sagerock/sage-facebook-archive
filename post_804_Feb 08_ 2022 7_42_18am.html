I am currently studying Forgiveness.

Forgiveness is an advanced placement course because it can seem like Acceptance.

Forgiveness is not acceptance of injustice. Forgiveness is not giving up and allowing oppression.

Forgiveness is letting go of hatred and anger. Forgiveness is standing against what is wrong while having love and compassion for the wrong doer.

I am in a unique position to show love and forgiveness while standing up for justice.

I hope to write more about this later.
