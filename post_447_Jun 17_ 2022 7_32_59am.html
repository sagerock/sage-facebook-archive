When this whole sex trafficking at our tent city came up in the news I think many people instantly thought: "Sage did it!"

I don't blame you in thinking that at all. You have been continually let down by your leaders. They all seem to be corrupt or evil or both.

But that's not true. Those are just the ones that make the news.

I have a cop friend that I want SO bad to tell you about. This guy could be from a super hero movie. He's SO great. But he doesn't want to be mentioned.

I make a point of showing you all my sides. I rage with anger. I swear constantly. I give zero fucks what kind of drugs a person jams into their body. I am all about women making a buck with their bodies. I used to drink so much I was actually hoping I'd die from it.

July 9 will be my 19 year anniversary for quitting drinking. I quit in 2003. After a DUI. After missing day after day of work. After being a dick to my family and friends. I finally quit. I've never touched it since.

I am not a saint. I am every bit as human as everyone else here. In fact, I pride myself on my earthy humanity. I don't want to be a saint. I just want to be a good human.

BUT THESE ARE FACTS ABOUT ME:
I don't buy drugs.
I don't sell drugs.
I don't buy sex.
I don't sell sex.

Those are facts that are absolutely true.

No one has contacted me from any police agency about this case. (The police officer friend I mentioned above called me yesterday about getting an ID for a homeless woman who stays with us. That's it. No one else.)

But maybe they are going to eventually. And I will happily talk to them.

If there was sex trafficking going on I want to know about it. I want it to end. I support women selling sex. I don't support exploiting women.

I personally now don't think there was. I believe it was Raven trying to hurt Celeste (2 fake named people I mentioned yesterday). I also heard Raven screaming that she was going to burn our camp to the ground. One time a homeless woman burned down our portapotty as she was leaving because we kicked her out. And another time a man reported people living in my building because "if he couldn't live there no one could."

There is so much pain and suffering in the homeless community. Taking it out on others unfortunately sometimes happens.

BUT ALL THAT SAID, I will gladly and happily work with anyone to go after someone that was trafficking a person. (And by trafficking I mean a person who forced a person to have sex against their will for money... and general variations on that theme.)

I will always do my best to be as honest and truthful about who I am with you. You might not like what you see. But at least you'll know it's the truth.

Have a great day.
Sage
