Sage Lewis Sleeping Out For Homeless People On Christmas Eve 2022
I wanted to remind you that I will be spending Christmas Eve sleeping in a tent at the corner of West Market and Portage Path from 6pm to 6am in solidarity with homeless pregnant women. People have slept out with me before. I really wish you wouldn't. It's not safe. And it is going to be 7 degrees. I CANNOT TAKE CARE OF YOU IN ANY WAY. But I would LOVE if you would stop by and come visit me. And, if you want, you can bring hats, gloves, hand warmers, zero degree sleeping bags, tents and tarps that I will give out to my homeless friends. I love you. And stay strong.
