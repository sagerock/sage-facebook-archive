I'm back to reading Thomas Merton. "Conjectures of a Guilty Bystander."

His words are the equivalent to a psychedelic drug. He says the most profound trippy truths. I wonder if Jim Morrison read any Thomas Merton.

His message is a drumbeat of emptying. Letting go. Removing things. Removing ego. You will not see God until it's all cleared away. And then there is only one way to unlock the door. You have to unlock it from the inside.
