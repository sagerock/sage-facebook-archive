HE TOOK HER SHOES.
HE TOOK HER SHOES. This clip from yesterday's homeless camp sweep in Akron Ohio represents my helplessness perfectly. For the last 8 years I've been screaming that there are American citizens abandoned on the streets of the richest, most Christian country in the world and no one cares. I just yell and yell and yell and they turn their back on me and just carry on with their phone conversations like everything is totally fine and that there is nothing to see here.
